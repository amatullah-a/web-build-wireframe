TY STEM Challenge: Astellas & J&J Visits

Our TY students taking part in the STEM TY Challenge enjoyed industry visits on Friday to Astellas and Johnson & Johnson. During the visits, students attended a presentation, took part in mentor interviews, and were given a tour of the sites. It was a fantastic opportunity to meet their mentors, gain an insight into what life in STEM is really like, and see how classroom learning connects with real industry.👏

Our Mission

Our school is a Catholic school, which aspires to the full development of all its members according to Christian principles as outlined in the CEIST Charter.

Every effort is made to ensure that each student develops according to his/her talents and potential: physically, spiritually, emotionally, and intellectually.

Our Vision & Values

We aim to develop fully integrated people who are aware of their own dignity as persons, who have Christian values, who are reliable, trustworthy, honest, truthful, caring, prayerful, devoted to duty, lovers of God and of neighbour; people who are prepared to reach out to those less fortunate than themselves.

How to Apply

Thank you for considering Presentation Secondary School for your child's secondary education. As a proud member of the Education and Training Board (ETB), our school is dedicated to providing a high-quality, inclusive, and supportive learning environment for all students.

At Presentation Secondary School, we believe in nurturing each student's unique talents and abilities. Our comprehensive curriculum, combined with a wide range of extracurricular activities, ensures that every student has the opportunity to excel academically, socially, and personally. We are committed to fostering a community where students feel valued, respected, and empowered to achieve their full potential.

Our dedicated and experienced staff work tirelessly to create a positive and engaging educational experience. We prioritise the well-being and development of our students, preparing them for future success in higher education, careers, and life.

We invite you to explore our admissions process and learn more about what makes Presentation Secondary School a special place for your child's education. Please feel free to contact us with any questions or to arrange a visit. We look forward to welcoming you to our school community.

Important dates:

  • Incoming first year's applications open October 1st of any given year. The closing date is October 31st.
  • Open night occurs in the 2nd week of October each year.
  • Enrollment for all other years requires contact to be made with the school secretary for the most up-to-date information.
